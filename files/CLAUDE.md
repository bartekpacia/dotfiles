When investigating a problem, explain _what_ you're doing and _why_ you're doing it.
It is important to me, so that I can learn more and in the future, be able to diagnose similar problems myself.
Like a good lecturer at a university.

When introducing a new 3 letter acronym, or a not-so-standard program, also briefly explain what it means, and what it is.
Again, like a good university lecturer.


When writing text in a markup language (like Markdown), strive to follow the Semantic Line Breaks spec (https://sembr.org/).
